Oda Sensei's Favorite Sea Chicken Onigiri

ingredints

  • 2 cups rice
  • 80g can tuna
  • 1 tbsp miso
  • 1/3 sugar
  • 5cm piece green onion
  • nori as needed
  • salt as nedded

How to make

  1. Finely chop the green onion
  2. Drain the tuna and put it in a frying pan over medium heat, along with the miso and sugar mix
  3. Add onion and mix
  4. Remove from heat (A).
  5. Dip hands in lightly salted water, grab a ball of rice and insert ingredients in the middle
  6. Close
  7. Shape into triangular onigiri form.
  8. Cut and attach nori as desired

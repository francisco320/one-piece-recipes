Kombu and Katsuo Broth

ingredints

  • makes ~1000 mL
  • 1200mL water
  • 1 (10x10) sheet kombu
  • 20g katsuobushi

How to make

  1. Soak kombu in water for at least 30 minutes
  2. Turn to medium heat and remove kombu just before boiling
  3. Add katsuobushi and remove from heat (3). When the katsuobushi sinks, strain.

Sanji’s Chicken Stock

ingredints

  • 2 wings of chicken bones/carcass
  • 1200mL water
  • 3000mL water
  • 150g vegetable bits (e.g. green parts of a leek, carrot skin, ginger peel)

How to make

  1. Rinse chicken with water and divide into 3 even pieces.
  2. Put into boiling water, when the surface becomes white, remove from water. Clean off any blood, etc
  3. Add water, chicken, and vegetables in a pot and heat on medium. When it reaches a boil, scoop froth from the top (3). Simmer 40-60 minutes on low (4). Taste. If the taste is like broth, it is done. Strain out vegetables bits and chicken (5).
